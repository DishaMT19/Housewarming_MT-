🔧 CONFIGURATION GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BEFORE GOING LIVE - DO THESE 3 THINGS:

1️⃣ SET HOUSE LOCATION
   In Settings → "📍 House Location"
   • Enter your home's latitude
   • Enter your home's longitude
   • Enter address (optional)
   • Click "💾 Save Location"
   
   Example locations:
   • Bangalore: 12.9716, 77.5946
   • Delhi: 28.6139, 77.2090
   • Mumbai: 19.0760, 72.8777
   • Hyderabad: 17.3850, 78.4867

2️⃣ SET HOST PHONE NUMBER
   In Settings → "☎️ Host Contact"
   • Enter phone number (with country code)
   • Click "💾 Save Phone"
   
   Format: +91 XXXXX XXXXX (or your country format)

3️⃣ REPLACE IMAGES & VIDEO
   Replace files in:
   • /assets/images/ → Keep filenames (home1.jpg - home6.jpg)
   • /assets/videos/journey.mp4 → Your video file
   
   Recommended formats:
   • Images: JPG, PNG (500x500px or larger)
   • Video: MP4 (720p or 1080p)
